Good Burger Recipe

Where did Burgers come from?

Burgers originated in a place called Hamburg, Germany. German immigrants arrived in New York and Chicago and began opening a restaurants. They sold what they call "hamburg" steaks which was an Americanized version of the German origin. During the industrial revolution, factory workers needed something quick and fast to eat. Thus the idea of food carts serving hamburgers was born. The ingenious idea of putting a hamburger steak between bread allowed factory workers to eat while on the go.

Ingredients of a good burger

  • Quality meat-typically 80-20 is a perfect ratio for making a burger
  • Fresh toppings(onion,tomato,lettuce)
  • A good burger sauce - mixing mayo and ketchup is usually suffice
  • American Cheese - although american cheese is not considered cheese it melts the best out of all the cheeses

How to make a good burger

  1. Start by creating a burger patty by mashing up ground beef until it's a little ball
  2. Make sure to get your pan nice and hot. Put the meat ball on the pan and smash down on the burger as flat as possible
  3. The burger will typically cook fast. After 3 minutes make sure to add cheese and let it stay on the grill for 1 more minute
  4. Make sure to get your bun nice and toasted and assemble your ingredients accordingly
  5. Serve with a side like french fries and enjoy!
